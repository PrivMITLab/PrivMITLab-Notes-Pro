## 🌟 Overview

**PrivMITLab-Notes-Pro** is a standalone, single-file offline note-taking application designed for individuals who demand absolute privacy and military-grade security for their personal thoughts and professional planning. No servers, no tracking, just your data, encrypted.

---

## 🚀 Pro Features (v4.0 Major Upgrade)

### 🔒 Uncompromising Security
*   **Zero-Knowledge Architecture:** All data is encrypted locally. Your master key never leaves your device's RAM.
*   **Military-Grade Encryption:** Industry-standard **AES-256-CBC** with 100,000 PBKDF2-SHA256 iterations.
*   **Privacy Guard:** Burn-after-reading notes, session auto-lock, and an emergency **NUKE** system.

### 🎙️ Advanced Voice Hub
*   **Pro Voice Selection:** Choose specifically between multiple natural Male/Female voices for both **Hindi (India)** and **English (US)**.
*   **Real-time Highlighting:** High-contrast yellow highlight follows the spoken word during playback.
*   **Smart Sync-Scroll:** The editor automatically scrolls to keep the active text in view.
*   **High-Resolution STT:** Professional-grade speech-to-text for hands-free dictation.

### ✍️ Professional Editor
*   **Markdown Pro:** Full GFM support, live preview, and syntax highlighting for 100+ languages.
*   **Custom Typography:** Switch between modern Sans-serif and professional Monospace (JetBrains Mono).
*   **Interactive Templates:** One-click presets for Project Plans, Weekly Reviews, and Meeting Agendas.
